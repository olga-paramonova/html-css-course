By day, the meeting rooms and picnic areas of Local County Zoo boast such unusual backdrops as elephant habitats and tropical gardens. By night, the sounds of exotic animals may be heard as guests arrive for their cocktail parties and wedding receptions. Event spaces are versatile in style and appearance, with capacities ranging from 10 guests to 500. Our Zoo holds an exclusive partnership with one of the country’s most innovative event specialist caterers, Service Systems Associates, Inc. ‘SSA’ is committed to quality, sustainability and supporting local farmers. With its headquarters located on Zoo premises, SSA can ensure smooth event planning and execution. In addition, a significant portion of your event’s proceeds support animal conservation, helping protect endangered animals and their habitats around the world.
